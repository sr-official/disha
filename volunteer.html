Join our volunteer network and become part of a movement that empowers youth, builds skills, and transforms communities.

About Volunteering

Volunteering at Disha gives you the opportunity to contribute to meaningful social work while developing your own skills. You will work with motivated teams on projects focused on education, digital empowerment, and community impact.

Every volunteer plays a role in shaping a better future.


Who Can Volunteer?

  • Young professionals
  • Students from any discipline
  • Socially motivated individuals
  • Anyone passionate about community development

Volunteer Roles

  • Field Research Assistance
  • Event and Program Support
  • Training & Workshop Support
  • Community Outreach Activities
  • Social Media & Content Creation

Benefits of Volunteering

  • Personalized Skills Report
  • Networking with changemakers
  • Personal growth and skill building
  • Leadership and teamwork development
  • Real-world experience in social projects
  • Research Publications and Acknowledgment
